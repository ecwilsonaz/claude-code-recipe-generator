# Macro Targets

Personalized targets for 2-meal-per-day keto protocol.

**Note:** All recipes follow her dietary requirements (low-histamine, low-FODMAP, APOE4-modified, higher fiber for IMO). Recipes are designed per-serving, then scaled.

---

## Per-Serving Targets

All recipes should target these macros **per serving**:

| Macro | Target | Range |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Calories | 900 | 850-1,000 |
| Protein | 55g | 50-65g |
| Fat | 65g | 60-75g |
| Net Carbs | 20g | 15-25g |
| Fiber | 15g | 12-18g |

---

## Scaling

| Scenario | Servings | Total Recipe |
|----------|----------|--------------|
| Cooking for one | 1x | ~900 cal / 55g protein |
| Cooking for two | 2x | ~1,800 cal / 110g protein |

**His adjustment:** Add 2 oz extra protein to his plate (+14g protein, +70 cal).

### Net Carbs (~20g per serving)

Net carbs come almost entirely from vegetables. No need to track separately if hitting vegetable/fiber targets.

| Vegetable | Amount | Net Carbs | Fiber |
|-----------|--------|-----------|-------|
| Broccoli | 1 cup | 4g | 5g |
| Brussels sprouts | 1 cup | 5g | 4g |
| Green beans | 1 cup | 4g | 4g |
| Zucchini | 1 cup | 3g | 2g |
| Carrots (cooked) | 1 cup | 9g | 4g |
| Bok choy | 1 cup | 1g | 1g |
| Bell peppers | 1 cup | 6g | 2g |

### Fiber (~15g per serving)

Target 2.5-3 cups cooked vegetables per serving:

| Combo | Amount | Fiber | Net Carbs |
|-------|--------|-------|-----------|
| Broccoli + Brussels sprouts | 1.5c + 1c | 12g | 11g |
| Broccoli + green beans + zucchini | 1c + 1c + 1c | 11g | 11g |
| Brussels sprouts + carrots + bok choy | 1.5c + 0.5c + 1c | 9g | 13g |

**To hit 15g fiber:** Add extra vegetables or include higher-fiber options like broccoli and Brussels sprouts. A generous 3 cups of mixed vegetables typically hits 12-15g.
