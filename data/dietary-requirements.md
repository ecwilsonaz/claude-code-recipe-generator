# Dietary Requirements for Recipe Generation

These constraints must be followed when generating any recipe.

---

## Proteins

| ✓ Allowed | ✗ Avoid |
|-----------|---------|
| Chicken (any cut) | Bacon |
| Turkey | Sausage |
| Duck | Deli meats |
| Lamb | Hot dogs |
| Pork (fresh, unprocessed) | Cured/smoked meats |
| Beef (fresh, limit frequency) | Canned fish (sardines, tuna, anchovies) |
| Eggs | Shellfish |
| Fresh fish (salmon, cod, halibut, etc.) | Smoked salmon |

**Protein note:** Assume all meat is fresh or frozen. Never use leftovers, aged, or canned proteins in recipes.

## Absolute Never Use

These ingredients must never appear in any recipe:

- Onion, garlic (except garlic-infused oil)
- Tomatoes or tomato products
- Avocado
- Spinach
- Eggplant
- Vinegar (any type)
- Soy sauce, fish sauce
- Bacon, sausage, deli meats
- Canned fish
- Shellfish
- Aged cheese
- Fermented foods
- Traditional slow-cooked bone broth (24+ hours)
- Sweeteners (all types — sugar, honey, maple syrup, artificial, sugar alcohols)

---

## Recipe Notes

- **Primary cooking fat:** Extra virgin olive oil or garlic-infused olive oil
- **Flavor without garlic/onion:** Use garlic-infused oil, ginger, scallion greens, fresh herbs, citrus zest
- **All proteins assumed fresh:** Do not suggest "leftover" anything
- **Cheese must be fresh:** Mozzarella, ricotta, paneer — never aged
- **No sweeteners:** Do not add any sweeteners to recipes
