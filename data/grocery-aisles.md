# Grocery Aisles

Customize this list for your store layout. The shopping list generator uses these aisles to organize items.

## Produce
Fresh fruits, vegetables, herbs, garlic, ginger, lemongrass, scallions, leafy greens, citrus

## Eggs and Non-Cheese Dairy
Eggs, butter, heavy cream, sour cream, yogurt, milk alternatives

## Cheese
All cheeses - hard, soft, shredded, cream cheese

## Meat, Freezer, and Cans
Fresh meat, poultry, seafood, frozen vegetables, canned tomatoes, coconut milk, broths, stocks

## Chips, Crackers, and Oils
Cooking oils, olive oil, avocado oil, sesame oil, vinegars, coconut aminos, hot sauce

## Bulk
Spices, dried herbs, nuts, seeds, almond flour, coconut flour
